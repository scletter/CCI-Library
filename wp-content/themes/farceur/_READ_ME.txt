
	FACUER THEME

	- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

	FARUCER theme is a wordpress theme for the Caring Clowns International
	organization.	Free of all presentational elements and non-semantic 
	markup. 

	- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

	FAURCER 1.1.0 is the version that is currently deployed.

	- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

	FAUCER 1.0.0 was the initial version created from scratch from the 
	prior Caring CLowns International theme.
	It was re-written to simplify and improve maintaineance.
	It was based on the original version of the 'Twenty Ten' theme (versions prior to 
	3.0 were based on the now-retired 'Default' theme) and a theme call
	Starkers.  Starkers was update through 4.0 and discontinued.

	- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

	Starkers Version 4.1 updated 6/6/09/2015 for CCI site 
	to support upgrades of Wordpress 4.3.1 since
	original theme not maintained.  Eliminated all
	twentyten references and updated for deprecated
	functions no longer supported.    There is also
	some specific theme files that support CCI unique
	pages.
